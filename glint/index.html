About

Glint is a simple rss reader inspired by the design of Layer Vault's Designer News and the chrome app Panda. It started off as a way to keep up with news emerging from the gaming industry.

Why the name 'Glint'? No reason really. I was inspired by the light that reflected off my glass of orange juice whilst at work and I thought glint would be a cool name for a project.

I'm not sure what the future of this side project will look like but I hope you have as much fun using it as I did making it. Hit me up on twitter or send me an email if you have any feature suggestions.
